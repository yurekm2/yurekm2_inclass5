The Hodag of Rhinelander

September 15th, 2025

Though highly debated, it is known that in the late 19th century, a land surveyor by the name of Eugene Shepherd stumbled across a fearsome beast while surveying the land of what is now present-day Rhinelander, Wisconsin. Shepherd's beast was photographed after knocking it out with chloroform, and it appears to be a spiny, furry, four legged creature, bearing large horns and fangs. Some describe it as a Godforsaken mashup of a dinosaur, a bear, and a bull.
